# Privacy Policy — StockPing

**Last updated: 2026-03-22**

StockPing does not collect, store, or transmit your spreadsheet data to any external server.

All settings are stored exclusively in Google Apps Script's PropertiesService (your Google account).

Notifications are sent only to destinations you configure. The developer does not receive copies.

License keys are validated locally via cryptographic hash. No external server call is made.

For questions: privacy@example.com
